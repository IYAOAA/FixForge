How to Stop Windows Auto Update in 2 Minutes

Posted on Jan 15, 2025 | By GizmoClinic Team

This step-by-step guide shows you how to disable Windows automatic updates in just two minutes. It’s quick, easy, and safe.

Steps:

  1. Open the Windows Settings app.
  2. Go to Update & Security.
  3. Click “Pause updates” or use the Group Policy Editor to disable.
